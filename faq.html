Where is the data stored?

Our data is currently stored in Amazon S3 in the United States. All content is legal under the US law. This means we may be forced to remove copyrighted or illegal material.

How do I use this service?

There are a ton of ways to hook into the API for OwO, if you're not very tech savvy (or just cant be bothered), there's a ShareX and MacOS script, however if you want to write your own, or your script isn't supported, please checkout the API here.

Private? I wanna upload!

Currently, we're not accepting new users on the ser

vice, as we're constantly making improvements to our backend and distribution infrastructure and configuration. Shoot us an email and we'll get back to you at a later date.

What's the maximum filesize I can upload?

Currently the max filesize that you can upload to our server is 80MiB. This is to prevent large files taking up a lot of space, and costing a lot to store.

What filetypes are allowed?

Currently, we have very strict filetype checking and only allow a small whitelist of files. You should be able to upload most image types and a few other types. If you would like us to allow another filetype, please send us an email. We hope to open this up when we leave beta.

I don't like something hosted here.

We're sorry to hear about that. If any images infringe on your rights or the rights of an entity you represent, please shoot us an email using the link in the footer. Make sure to provide us with the URL(s) of the files in question.
